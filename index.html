Hi, I am Danny Tejeda. A robotics engineer born and raised in Mexico. Currently based in the Los Angeles Area.

I love research, design, and robots!

I am pursuing a career in robotic development and ocean engineering. I'm deeply curious about sampling techniques and monitoring methods for wildlife in the deep ocean with Remote Operated Vehicles and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles. 71% of the Earth is covered with water, but we have only explored 5% and there are still many questions that can only be answered as the limits of technology allows us to acquire data. I want to be at the forefront of innovation to help scientists get more accurate information about the ocean.
